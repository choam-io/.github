# CHOAM

**Combine Honnete Ober Advancer Mercantiles**

We are a privately held technology group focused on research, infrastructure, and enabling tools for distributed systems. Our work spans applied cryptography, developer tooling, and exploratory projects at the intersection of coordination and computation.

CHOAM operates as a holding structure for independent technical initiatives. We invest in long-term problems and maintain a portfolio of open-source and internal projects.
